Miso Soup

Miso soup is the most commonly eaten traditional soup dish in Japan

This soup is extremely versatile. It can be eaten with wakame seaweed, tofu, green onions, tofu, small clams, and many more ingredients. Today, we'll learn how to make a simple one.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups water
  • miso paste
  • tofu
  • green onions
  • wakame seaweed

Steps

  1. bring the water to a boil
  2. reduce the heat and add miso paste to taste(the longer you boil it the saltier it gets)
  3. dice the tofu and put it in along with green onions and wakame seaweed
  4. ready to serve after a couple of mins
